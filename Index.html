Our Story

Where Passion
Meets the Pour

Born from a deep love for the ritual of coffee, Coffee O' Clock was founded with a simple belief: every cup should be an experience worth savoring. We source our beans from single-origin farms across Colombia, Ethiopia, and Sumatra — roasted in-house for peak flavor.

Our baristas are craftspeople. Each drink is made with precision, patience, and a touch of artistry that you can taste from the very first sip.
